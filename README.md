# TradeMindLog

**TradeMindLog** is a specialized investment logging application designed to capture the "why" behind every transaction. Unlike standard portfolio trackers that only focus on balances, this app digitizes your decision-making process to help you refine your investment strategy over time.

---

## Concept
**"Log the mind, not just the trade."**  
TradeMindLog is built on the philosophy that the value of an investment log lies in documenting the information and rationale available at the moment of execution. By recording your psychological state and logic, you can objectively review whether a trade was a result of a sound strategy or an emotional impulse.

## Problems it Solves
Most investors struggle with the following:
*   **Memory Decay:** Forgetting the specific catalysts that led to a buy or sell order.
*   **Lack of Post-Mortem:** An inability to verify if a sale was "too early" or "too late" months after the fact.
*   **Hidden Causality:** Difficulty identifying the root causes of recurring profits or losses.

**TradeMindLog** transforms these ephemeral thoughts into structured data for continuous improvement.

## Target Audience
*   **Individual Stock Investors:** From beginners to intermediate traders looking for a systematic edge.
*   **Experimental Investors:** Perfect for those managing high-volume portfolios (e.g., "100-stock experiments") who need to track diverse rationales.
*   **Growth-Oriented Traders:** Anyone focused on the "Plan-Do-Check-Act" (PDCA) cycle for their wealth management.

## Key Features
*   **Transaction Contextualization:** Record not only price and volume but also the emotional reasons behind your trades.
*   **Performance Review:** Help you analyze the gap between your expectations and reality by looking back and analyzing your past trades.
